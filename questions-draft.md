
# Hair Quiz

Types
1. Straight
2. Wavy
3a. Curly
3c. Very curly
4. Coily

Format
- answer (types to give points to), rules out

1. Does your hair dry straight without needing products, heat, or tools?
	- Yes it dries straight or mostly straight (go to 1)
	- Yes, but it's kind of frizzy (1, 2), rules out 3 or 4
	- No
2. What happens when you try to brush your hair when it's dry?
	- It smoothes it out (1,2), rules out 4
	- It gets frizzy or poofy (2,3a), rules out 4 and 1
	- Can't brush my hair dry ( 3c, 4), rules out 1 and 2
	- I can brush my hair dry but it feels damaging (3,4)
3. Do you have a lot of trouble keeping your hair moisturized?
	- Y (3a, 3c, 4)
  - N (1,2)
4. Do you experience shrinkage (where your hair is much longer wet than dry)?
	- Yes a lot (3c,4) rules out 1 and 2
	- A fair bit (3a, 3c, 4)
	- Just a little (2,3a)
	- No (1,2) rules out 3 and 4
5. Do oily products make your hair limp?
	- Yes (1, 2,3a) rules out 4
	- No (3c,4)
6. What's your biggest hair worry when sleeping?
	- Breakage (3c, 4)
	- Curls/waves loosening (2,3a) rules out 4
	- I don't have any worries
7. Do you need a lot of hold products to keep your curls/waves from loosening out?
  - No (3c, 4)
  - Yes (2,3a) Rules out 4
  - I'm not sure
9. Do you especially have trouble getting volume at your roots?
	- No (3c,4)
  - Yes (2, 3a)
10. How is your experience growing your hair out?
	- I haven't tried
	- I have trouble getting length (4, 3c)
	- It grows out easily (1,2,3a) rules out 4
	- It grows out but the curls/waves are less strong when it's long (2, 3a) rules out 4
11. Have you tried heatless curls?
  - No why would I need more curls? (3a, 3c, 4) rules out 1,2
  - Yes, and they worked well (2,3a, 3,b)
  - Yes, but they were kind of weak (1,2)
  - Yes, and they didn't work at all (1)
  - No, I haven't tried
12. When you stretch out your hair is it much longer than it is resting?
  - No (1,2) rules out 4
  - it's longer (2,3a)
  - A lot longer (3c,4)
